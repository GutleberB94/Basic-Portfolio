My Story



I was born and raised in Middletown, New Jersey. There I grew up enjoying the many parks, beaches and local restaurants. Also taking part in many different hobbies as I grew up from sports like baseball and football, to skateboarding and snowboarding. However, from a very young age I have always had an interest in technology. It started out with the amasement I had with some of the earlier PC video games. This led me to start tinkering with the computer my Dad had given me to upgrade its hardware. Eventually getting to build my first computer from scratch in middle school. After spending years learning about hardware and going to school for networking and system administration roles I decided to give programming a try. Spending some time getting familiar with the syntax, printing "Hello World" to the screen in python blew my mind. From that moment I knew I wanted to pursue a career in software of web development.
